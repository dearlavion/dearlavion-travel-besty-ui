Our Story
Why Travel Besty exists

Some people count sheep. I count layovers I'd take again just for the window seat.

I've always been the person who gets to the airport two hours early — not because I have to, but because I like being there. The smell of coffee at the gate, the boarding announcements, watching a plane push back from the jet bridge. It never got old.

But every trip started the same way: scattered lists, half-packed bags, and that nagging feeling of "did I forget something?" I'd overpack for short trips and underpack for long ones. I'd buy a $12 travel pillow at the airport because I forgot mine again.

So I started building my own systems — a list for the beach, a different one for the mountains, one for quick weekend trips, another for the ones where I'd be gone a month. Friends started asking me to help them pack for their trips too. That's where the name came from: I wanted to be the friend who already has your trip figured out before you even finish asking.

Travel Besty started as a favor for friends. Now it's a place where anyone can answer a few simple questions and get a kit built just for their trip — the way I wish someone had done for me on my very first flight.

— The Founder
Frequent flyer, list-maker, window-seat loyalist
